Vehicles

There is a total of 15 vehicles in the Lark-Space Inventory and these can all be categorized in 1 of 3 groups

St Jude(Space Station):

  • Specifically designed for both astronauts and their families
  • This is a space station that floats in space and is used solely for research purposes
  • Employees on this ship will have contracts ranging from 8 months to 36 months
  • Due to the extensive nature of these contracts, employees are allowed to bring their families; however, this must be discussed before the signing of their contract in order to make the correct accomidations
  • This station is a permanent structure in space and transportation to and from St Jude is provided from one of the Jemison Series ships
